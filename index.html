Rythm Charitable Society is a NGO based on Ponnani,Kerala. Establised on April 2016, our team has currently 70+ volunteers working on its behalf to find out the needs and necessities of people around them and to provide with the solution for it.

From providing food to the disabled people of Karunya Pain and Palliative Care, Tirur, to providing blankets to the people who are living in the streets of ponnani and kuttipuram.

We work for the betterment of people throughout the state. We conduct many programs with special children. The program will be a mixture of games and entertainment for the kids, cultural activities and exhibition of their drawings, session for their parents and oppurtunity to share their experience.

Volunteer Participation and Team Activities

Volunteers are the strength of our team. We are improving our activities day by day and most of our programs focus on disabled children and lifting their talents.
